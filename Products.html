Our Products

We invest time and expertise to fully understand your business before designing plans to improve your supply chain. We take responsibility for the performance of all our suppliers and for ensuring the availability of resources and equipment needed to control the flow of goods under our charge.

Machinery

Earth Moving Vehicles: We provide top-quality earth moving vehicles, including excavators, bulldozers, loaders, graders, and more. These robust machines are designed to handle heavy-duty tasks with precision and efficiency.


Construction Machinery/Equipment: Our range of construction machinery and equipment includes concrete mixers, cranes, pavers, scaffolding, and other essential tools for construction projects of all sizes.


Agriculture Machinery: We offer a comprehensive range of agricultural machinery, such as tractors, harvesters, irrigation systems, and planting equipment. These advanced machines enhance productivity and efficiency in the agricultural sector.


Industrial Machinery: From manufacturing equipment to specialized industrial machinery, we supply a wide range of machines for different industrial applications. This includes packaging machinery, CNC machines, textile machinery, and more.

Spares

We understand the importance of reliable and efficient equipment operation. That's why we provide a comprehensive range of spares for various industries, including:


Automotive Spares & Lubricants: We offer high-quality automotive spares and lubricants, ensuring optimal performance and longevity for vehicles of all types.


Earthmoving Spares: Our selection of earthmoving spares includes parts for excavators, loaders, bulldozers, and other heavy equipment. These genuine spares help maintain the efficiency and reliability of your machinery.


Machinery Spares: For industrial machinery and equipment, we supply a wide range of spares, including parts for CNC machines, industrial pumps, conveyors, and more. These spares are sourced from trusted manufacturers to ensure compatibility and durability.


Industrial Spares: From bearings and belts to valves and filters, our industrial spares cover a wide range of components used in manufacturing and industrial processes. These high-quality spares help optimize operations and minimize downtime.


Electric & Electronic Spares: We provide spares for electric and electronic components, including circuit boards, sensors, connectors, and more. These spares ensure the smooth functioning of electrical systems and equipment.

Construction Material

For construction projects, we offer a comprehensive range of construction materials, including:


All Types of Tiles: Whether it's ceramic, porcelain, mosaic, or natural stone tiles, we provide a wide selection of high-quality tiles for floors, walls, and other surfaces.


Sanitary Ware & Bath Fittings: Our collection includes sanitary ware such as toilets, basins, bidets, and bath fittings such as faucets, showers, and accessories. These products combine aesthetics and functionality to enhance your bathroom spaces.


Kitchenware: We offer a range of kitchenware items, including sinks, faucets, cabinets, countertops, and other essentials for modern and efficient kitchens.


Cement, Steel & Other Materials: We supply cement, steel, and other essential construction materials to meet the demands of various construction projects. Our products adhere to industry standards and ensure durability and strength.

Other Products

In addition to machinery and construction materials, we cater to a diverse range of other products, including:


Spices & Pulses: We provide a variety of aromatic spices and high-quality pulses sourced from trusted suppliers. These products add flavor and richness to culinary preparations.


Agro, Dairy & Processed Foods: Our range includes agro products such as grains, fruits, and vegetables, as well as dairy products and processed foods. These products meet stringent quality standards and offer nutrition and taste.


Organic, Herbal & Cosmetic Products: We offer organic and herbal products, including skincare, haircare, and personal care items. These natural products promote wellness and beauty in a sustainable manner.


Minerals, Chemicals & Allied Products: Our selection includes minerals, chemicals, and allied products for various industrial applications. These products are sourced from reliable manufacturers to ensure quality and compliance.


All Types of New Vehicles: From cars and motorcycles to trucks and commercial vehicles, we supply a wide range of new vehicles from reputable manufacturers. Our vehicles meet safety standards and provide reliable transportation solutions.


All Kinds of Electric & Electronics Goods: We offer a diverse range of electric and electronic goods, including home appliances, consumer electronics, IT equipment, and more. These products combine cutting-edge technology with reliability and performance.


Furniture: Our furniture collection includes a variety of styles and designs for residential, commercial, and hospitality settings. From office furniture to home furnishings, we offer functional and aesthetically pleasing options.


Air Conditioning Solutions: We provide air conditioning systems and solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our range includes energy-efficient systems that ensure optimal comfort and climate control.


Textiles & Garments: We supply textiles and garments, including fabrics, clothing, and accessories. From traditional wear to modern fashion, our products cater to diverse preferences and trends.
