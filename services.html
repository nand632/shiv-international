Our Services

At Shiv International, we offer a comprehensive range of services to facilitate smooth and efficient import-export operations for our clients. With our expertise, global network, and commitment to excellence, we provide reliable solutions tailored to meet your specific needs. From sea cargo and air cargo to courier services, our services cover all aspects of the import-export process.

Sea Cargo

Our sea cargo services provide reliable and cost-effective transportation solutions for your goods across international waters. We handle both full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL), ensuring that your shipments are handled with utmost care and delivered to their destination on time.

Documentation and Customs Clearance: We assist with all necessary documentation, including bill of lading, commercial invoices, packing lists, and customs declarations. Our team ensures compliance with import/export regulations and smooth customs clearance.

Container Loading and Unloading: We manage the loading and unloading of containers, optimizing space utilization and ensuring secure packaging to protect your goods during transit.

Freight Forwarding: Our experienced freight forwarding team works closely with reliable shipping lines to negotiate competitive rates and secure timely shipping schedules. We provide end-to-end logistics coordination to streamline the sea cargo process.

Tracking and Monitoring: We provide real-time tracking and monitoring of your shipments, keeping you informed about the status and location of your goods throughout the journey.

Air Cargo

When time is of the essence, our air cargo services offer fast and efficient transportation solutions. We leverage our partnerships with major airlines to provide reliable and expedited air freight services. Our air cargo services include:

Air Freight Forwarding: We handle all aspects of air freight forwarding, from booking cargo space to arranging customs clearance and documentation. Our team ensures that your goods are delivered swiftly and securely.

Express and Consolidation Services: Whether you have time-sensitive shipments that require express delivery or multiple shipments that can be consolidated for cost savings, we provide flexible air cargo solutions to meet your requirements.

Customs Compliance: Our experts have in-depth knowledge of customs regulations and procedures, ensuring smooth customs clearance for your air cargo shipments.

Priority Handling: We prioritize the handling of your air cargo shipments, ensuring quick turnaround times and efficient handling at both origin and destination airports.

Courier Services

For small packages and documents that require urgent delivery, we offer reliable courier services through our partnerships with renowned courier companies such as FedEx, DHL, and Aramex. Our courier services provide:

Door-to-Door Delivery: We offer seamless door-to-door delivery services, ensuring that your packages reach their intended recipients quickly and safely.

Express and Standard Options: Whether you need express delivery for time-sensitive shipments or cost-effective standard delivery, we provide flexible options to meet your specific requirements.

Tracking and Proof of Delivery: We provide tracking facilities and proof of delivery for your courier shipments, allowing you to monitor the progress and ensure the successful delivery of your packages.

Packaging and Labeling: Our team ensures that your packages are appropriately packaged and labeled to meet courier company requirements, ensuring smooth processing and delivery.

At Shiv International, we understand the importance of reliable and efficient logistics in the import-export business. With our extensive experience and commitment to customer satisfaction, we ensure that your goods are transported safely and efficiently to their destination. Contact us today to discuss your import-export requirements and discover how our services can benefit your business.
